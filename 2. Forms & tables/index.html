an easy step-by-step sandwich recipe for beginners

Ingredients:

  1. 2 slices of bread (white, wheat, or multigrain)
  2. 1 tablespoon butter or cheese spread (any spread of choice)
  3. 1 teaspoon green chutney (store-bought or homemade)
  4. 1 tomato, thinly sliced
  5. 1 cucumber, thinly sliced
  6. Pinch of salt and pepper (optional)

instructions:

  1. Prep the Bread
    Take two slices of bread. On one slice, evenly spread butter or cheese spread. On the second slice, spread green chutney.
  2. Layer the Vegetables
    Lay the tomato slices evenly over the buttered slice. Place the cucumber slices on top of the tomato. Sprinkle a little salt and pepper if desired.
  3. Assemble the Sandwich
    Top with the second slice of bread (the one with chutney). Press gently so the sandwich sticks together.
  4. Serve or Pack
    Cut the sandwich diagonally for easy handling and pack if carrying for later. Serve fresh, as is, or with ketchup or extra chutney.
